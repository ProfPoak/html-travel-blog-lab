Adventures in Fire Island

Fire island is a once in a lifetime trip. The sandy shores and luscious grass are breathtaking. No one should ever skip the opportunity see a sunrise with the waves crashing at your feet. For anyone on the fence, stop thinking and go. The relaxation is unparalleled. Future you will thank you for making the decision. The lighthouse is a beautiful landmark for anyone history buffs.
